# Recommendation Memo

## Executive Summary

The company tested a new onboarding and activation campaign against the existing onboarding experience. The objective was to improve user conversion and engagement while monitoring potential risks.

## North Star Metric

Paid Conversion Rate

## KPI Tree Explanation

The KPI tree includes Acquisition, Activation, and Engagement drivers that support Paid Conversion Rate. Guardrail metrics were monitored to ensure business growth does not negatively impact user experience.

## Experiment Results

Paid conversion rate increased from 3.17% in the Control group to 6.99% in the Treatment group.

Landing page visit rate increased from 63.64% to 72.59%.

Trial start rate increased from 25.11% to 29.09%.

Onboarding completion rate increased from 15.58% to 21.26%.

Average engagement score increased from 57.03 to 62.93.

Average revenue per user increased from 51.75 to 53.88.

## Hypothesis Test Interpretation

The treatment group outperformed the control group on the primary success metric. The experiment suggests that the new onboarding campaign is more effective at converting users into paying customers.

## Guardrail Analysis

Refund rate increased slightly from 0.00% to 0.42%.

Support ticket rate increased from 0.22 to 0.37.

Days to convert improved from 8.86 days to 6.40 days.

Although some guardrail metrics increased, the overall business impact remains positive.

## Segment-Level Insights

The treatment group outperformed the control group across all regions.

Mobile users showed the strongest improvement among device types.

Referral traffic generated the highest conversion uplift.

Social traffic showed slightly lower performance under treatment and should continue to be monitored.

## Final Recommendation

Launch the new onboarding experience to all users.

## Risks and Limitations

Some missing values and duplicate user IDs were identified during data quality checks. Revenue outliers were retained as valid observations.

## Next Steps

Monitor refund rate and support ticket rate after launch.

Continue optimizing the onboarding experience.

Investigate social traffic performance through additional experiments.
